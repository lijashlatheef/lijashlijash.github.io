About

Welcome To Our Industry

We Have 63 Years of Experience in this are, so you Can Espect From Us Better Services

Al Naime Trading Building Materials is a Free Zone company based in Jebel Ali, Dubai. We established in Dubai in 2004 but our roots are there since 1956 in Baghdad, Iraq "Halaat Al Shams Co."

We became the largest stock holders by keeping at least 70,000 metric tons of building materials in a more than 100,000 square meters stores in Iraq. This is possible because of the cooperation of 60 employees running the establishment to be the leading company in the Middle East..

"Akram Al Neami Building Materials Trading Co" is our sister company that is responsible for our MENA region logistics.

Our company offers a variety of building materials ranging from Pipes, Valves, Fittings and Flanges. All materials are from the best mills in Ukraine, Spain, Italy, Austria, Turkey, Oman, India and China. Our products are stored according to the best standards and specifications, all of which comes with Material Test Certificate (MTC).

Al Naime Trading Building Material FZCO is an authorized and exclusive distributor of the best quality mills around the world for different needs.
